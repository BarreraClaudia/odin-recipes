Shrimp Tacos

Description

These shrimp tacos are packed with flavor!

Ingredients

  • mini flour tortillas
  • cooking oil
  • 1 pound shrimp
  • 1 tsp paprika powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • 1 cup chopped cabbage
  • 1 chopped avocado
  • 1 lime

Steps

  1. Season and fry shrimp.
  2. Place shrimp on warm mini tortillas.
  3. Top with cabbage, avocado, and lime juice.
