Tinga

Description

Tinga is a mexican dish. It is shredded chicken in a chipotle tomato sauce. Usually served on top of a tostada.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • cooking oil
  • 1/2 onion
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 can chipotle in adobo sauce
  • salt
  • pepper
  • tostadas
  • chopped lettuce
  • sliced tomato

Steps

  1. Cook and shred chicken. Or you can be lazy like me and buy a rotisserie chicken.
  2. Fry the onion in whichever oil you prefer.
  3. Blend tomatoes and chipotle, then pour on top of onions.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Mix in shredded chicken.
  6. Serve onto tostadas and top with lettuce and tomatoes.
