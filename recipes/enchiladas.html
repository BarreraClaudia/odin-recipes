Enchiladas

Description

These enchiladas are creamy and cheesy!

Ingredients

  • corn tortillas
  • shredded cheese
  • 1 can cream of mushroom
  • 1 can cream of chicken
  • sliced jalapeno

Steps

  1. In a deep baking dish, place rolled up tortillas filled with cheese.
  2. Blend cream of mushroom and cream of chicken and pour on top of tortillas.
  3. Sprinkle cheese and jalapenos on top.
  4. Bake in oven until crispy.
